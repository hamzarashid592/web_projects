My tenure primarily centered around pivotal assignments at Meezan Bank Limited (MBL), the largest Islamic bank in Pakistan. Throughout this period, I spearheaded various Requests for Change (RFCs) that aimed at revolutionizing banking operations:

  • MBL Direct Biller:

    This RFC aimed to establish direct communication between the bank and billing companies, eliminating the need for a billing aggregator. This streamlined process resulted in significant savings for both parties by eliminating per transaction fees previously routed to the aggregator.

  • MBL Raast/MPG Phase II:

    This initiative aimed to introduce P2P internet banking payments using an easily memorable alias rather than conventional account numbers or IBANs. Enabling customers to transact using the beneficiary’s cell number simplified the payment process, aligning with the State Bank of Pakistan's mandates to digitize and centralize the country’s banking industry.
    MPG or Raast itself is sort of a country switch deployed at SBP. For more information click here.

  • MBL Wemsol Integration:

    Tasked with integrating Keenu Wallet, a Digital Wallet service by Wemsol Private Limited, with ATM machines. This venture provided insights into ATM functionalities, communication with controllers, and middleware interactions. Since Keenu uses biometrics for customer authentication, my focus centered on biometric flow within the ATM context.

  • ATM Biometric Activation:

    To bolster security and prevent fraud in ATM biometric transactions, I facilitated the implementation of OTP authentication alongside biometric entry, enhancing transaction security protocols.

  • Wallet Limit Segregation:

    Implemented measures to reduce IBFT fraud by segregating transfer limits between normal bank accounts and their wallet counterparts, mitigating potential fraudulent activities.

  • Nayapay Onus Acquiring:

    Facilitated Nayapay Visa/Paypak cardholders to make withdrawals via MBL ATMs by establishing direct communication between MBL and Nayapay through their exposed APIs, bypassing the involvement of intermediary networks like 1Link.
These initiatives underscored the commitment to innovation and efficiency within the banking sector, enabling streamlined processes and enhanced security measures for financial transactions.
